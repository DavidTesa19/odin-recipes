Chicken Steaks Recipe

Description

Chicken Steaks are just my most favourite food

It's easy to make and it's just brilliant, not suitable to vegans though

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts
  • Oil
  • Gyros seasoning or other

Steps

  1. Prepare Ingredients
  2. Cut chicken breasts into pieces
  3. Mix oil with seasoning
  4. Put chicken pieces into mix and mix it
  5. Fry it on a pan

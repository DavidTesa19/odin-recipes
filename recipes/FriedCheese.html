Fried Cheese Recipe

Description

Fried Cheese is a speciality very popular in Czech Republic

It's usually made of Eidam or Gouda cheese in triangualr or rectangle form

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Oil or butter
  • Bread
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Prepare Ingredients
  2. Mix eggs
  3. Make powder from bread
  4. Mix Eggs with bread powder
  5. Put Cheese into the mix
  6. Fry it
